Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups of Paella Rice
  • 1/2 cup of Olive Oil
  • 4 cups of Chicken Stock
  • 1 Onion, finely chopped
  • 1 Red Bell Pepper, chopped
  • 4 Garlic Cloves, minced
  • 1.5 lbs of Chicken Thighs
  • 1/2 lb of Chorizo
  • 1 cup of Green Peas
  • 1 Tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp of Saffron
  • 1 tsp of Paprika
  • 1 lb of Shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/2 lb of Mussels
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large paella pan or an equivalent.
  2. Add the chicken thighs and chorizo and fry until browned.
  3. Add the finely chopped onion, chopped bell pepper, and minced garlic cloves to the pan and cook until softened.
  4. Add the paella rice to the pan and stir well to coat in the oil.
  5. Pour in the chicken stock, add the saffron, paprika, and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook for 20 minutes, or until the rice is cooked.
  7. Add the shrimp, mussels, and green peas to the pan and cook for another 10 minutes, or until the seafood is cooked through.
  8. Finally, add the chopped tomato to the pan, stir well, and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  9. Remove the pan from the heat and let it sit for 5 minutes before serving.

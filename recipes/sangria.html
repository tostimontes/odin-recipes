Refreshing sangria

Ingredients

  • 1 bottle red wine
  • 1 orange (sliced)
  • 1 lemon (sliced)
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 shot brandy
  • 2 cups soda water

Steps

  1. Pour the bottle of red wine into a large pitcher.
  2. Add sliced orange and lemon to the pitcher.
  3. Add 2 tablespoons of sugar and stir until dissolved.
  4. Add a shot of brandy to the mix.
  5. Stir in soda water.
  6. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours before serving.
